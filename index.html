Who We Are

About Us

Persian Esports is a competitive gaming organization founded on a simple belief: talent deserves a stage, and passion deserves a team. We bring together elite players, dedicated staff, and a growing global community under one banner.

From grassroots scrims to international tournaments, our mission is to build sustainable, professional esports rosters that compete at the highest level while staying true to the culture that shaped us.

We're more than an org — we're a home for competitors, creators, and fans who live and breathe the game.
